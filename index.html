AI Models

Gemini 3.5 & Omni — Next-Gen AI Models

Google introduced the Gemini 3.5 family and the entirely new Omni world-model, combining frontier intelligence with speed and multimodal creation. Gemini 3.5 Flash is available now; Pro arrives next month.

New Model · Available Now

Gemini 3.5 Flash

Google's first model combining frontier intelligence with action — designed for agentic tasks, coding, and long-horizon workflows at 4× the speed of comparable frontier models.

  • Beats Gemini 3.1 Pro on coding & agents: 76.2% vs 70.3% Terminal-Bench 2.1
  • 4× faster than comparable frontier models; 1M-token context window; 64K output tokens
  • Pricing: $1.50/$9.00 per 1M tokens ($0.15 cached); knowledge cutoff: Jan 2026
  • API model ID: gemini-3.5-flash — GA, no preview suffix
  • Dynamic thinking on by default; text + image + audio + video inputs
  • Saves top enterprises $1B+ annually; Google processes 3T tokens/day internally

Release: June 2026 AP News

The most ambitious consumer AI product Google has ever shipped — a cloud-based personal agent running continuously, even when your laptop is closed or phone locked.

  • Powered by Gemini 3.5 Flash + Antigravity harness
  • Integrates with Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Drive + Canva, Instacart (more via MCP)
  • Access via Gemini app, email, chat — Chrome integration late summer
  • Android Halo: live task progress UI in Android OS (later 2026)

  • Asks permission before high-stakes actions (emails, purchases)
  • AI Ultra price: reduced to $200/mo (from $250); new $100/mo entry plan
